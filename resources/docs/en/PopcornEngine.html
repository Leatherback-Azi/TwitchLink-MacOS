Popcorn Engine


Suitable for special use to minimize data loss and receive stable data.


Can only download videos from the channel video list.
Unmute the video as much as possible and reduce data loss.
Supports Super Fast Download.
Crop not supported.
Downloading and encoding are performed sequentially.
Downloading and encoding are handled separately, requiring as much temporary storage as the final video.
Deletes all temporary data if cancelled during download.
The video encoded up to that point will be saved if cancelled during encoding.

 

Super Fast Download

Downloads data super fast.
It consumes heavy traffic and can cause buffering for other tasks that use the network, such as YouTube or Twitch.

 

Update Tracking Mode

It downloads the final data of the video that changes.
Live broadcast replays that are saved in real time receive additional video data.
Live broadcasts in which replays are saved can be downloaded by selecting the live broadcast from the replay list.
After the download is complete, the task of checking additional video files continues for 5 minutes.
It downloads stably stored data on the server without being affected by buffering or interruption that occurs during broadcast.

 

Normal Download Mode

Downloads the video as it is at the time of download.
The live replay, which is stored in real time, downloads only videos up to that point.
Encoding will proceed immediately after downloading is complete.
It downloads stably stored data on the server without being affected by buffering or interruption that occurs during broadcast.
