Biscuit Engine


Suitable for most general use.


Available for downloading both live broadcasts and videos from video lists.
Does not recover data in the muted part.
Super Fast Download not supported.
Supports partial download of video.
Downloading and encoding are performed simultaneously.
The video encoded up to that point will be saved if cancelled.

 

Live Download

Downloads live broadcast data transmitted in real time.
Live broadcasts that do not save replays can also be downloaded.
Affected by buffering or interruption that occurs during broadcast.

 

Video Download

Downloads the video as it is at the time of download.
The live replay, which is stored in real time, downloads only videos up to that point.
It downloads stably stored data on the server without being affected by buffering or interruption that occurs during broadcast.
